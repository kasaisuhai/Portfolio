Web Designer & Developer

I'm Bradyn Heaton, a Web Designer & Developer with a focus on frontend. While I'm familiar with the whole design process from research to development, I'm particularly good at prototyping, testing, and developing designs. I have an eye for small details, and I enjoy making sure that everything is precisely where it needs to be. I love taking a design through different iterations and seeing it change little by little until it becomes the final design.

Design

My design process begins with understanding the needs of the client & users, and identifying the problem. Once the problem is identified, I brainstorm solutions and try out different designs, testing each iteration until I hone in on the best solution.
